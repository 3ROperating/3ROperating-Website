About Ridge Runner Holdings, LLC

Ridge Runner Holdings, LLC is a privately held oil and natural gas company with offices in both Midland and Houston, Texas. Ridge Runner acquires, develops, and produces oil and gas properties in the Permian Basin and other domestic resource plays.
