Ridge Runner Resources II was formed in 2023 with an initial capital commitment from EnCap Investments L.P.


Since 1988, EnCap has raised 24 institutional funds totaling more than $40 billion and currently manages capital on behalf of more than 350 U.S. and international investors.

EnCap has a proven track record, having invested with more than 275 upstream and midstream companies. Throughout its history, EnCap has moved with the market to meet the changing capital needs of its clients. The combination of abundant capital resources, unparalleled experience and broad flexibility in structuring transactions uniquely positions EnCap to assist significantly in the process of value creation.
